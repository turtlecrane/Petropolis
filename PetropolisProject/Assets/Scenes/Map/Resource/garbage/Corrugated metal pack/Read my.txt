
The letters "A", "B", "C" stands for textures group. 
"A C" models use material with "A" textures on one material slot,and "C" textures material on the other slot.

"Rust patterns" are PNG images of rust, with alpha channel. You can use them to create a rusted paint efect.      

Use grid snap, and 90 degree rotation to easily create constructions (except the doors).

Use double sided materials/normals or similar function.
